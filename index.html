Global Azure Bootcamp on April 22, 2017!

Welcome to GABC!

Global Azure Bootcamp (#globalazure) is turning five years old and is a community owned and community driven day of Azure all around the world. On the same day, in 2017 that day is April 22, hundreds of user groups and tech communities run a one day, free to attend, meetup (or bootcamp) where we learn together about Azure. Each UG will arrange the agenda for the day, and with the content that makes sense, for them. Globally we arrange for sponsors with nice giveaways.
